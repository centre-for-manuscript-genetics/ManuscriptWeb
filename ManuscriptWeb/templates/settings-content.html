Colours and Design

System Colours

ManuscriptWeb follows a simple design approach, based on very few colours. This allows users with rather little experience in web design to costumize the environment.

Select two colours, preferably in the same area of the colour palette (e.g. a bright and a slightly darker green).

The following colours are used for the backgrounds. To achieve a good design, one should be dark and the other one light. Both should contrast your colour choices above.

System Fonts

ManuscriptWeb works with GoogleFonts. You can choose three fonts to customize your website. Each selection is applied to the system instantly to allow you to see how it affects the design of your ManuscriptWeb instance.

The Navigation Font is used for all navigations within your system. This applies to the main navigation on top of the website as well as all side navigations such as the one on this Settings page. While all fonts you chose should be pleasantly legible, you might use a more experimental font than for your Default Font, as the navigations do not contain longer text.
