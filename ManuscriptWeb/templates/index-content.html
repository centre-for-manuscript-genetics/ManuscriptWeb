DanSeq, genetic edition of Aidan Chambers’ Dance Sequence novels

Aidan Chambers published the six novels of his Dance Sequence between 1978 and 2006, but he began working on Breaktime in 1975 and Dance on My Grave as early as 1967.

The DanSeq project reads the Dance Sequence novels from their earliest conception up until their date of publication. It gives Chambers’ readers a new way of entering the Dance Sequence novels. It collates and chronologises the existing versions of Chambers’ Dance Sequence novels.

DanSeq is pronounced [ˈtænˀsk], incidentally like dansk, the Danish and Norwegian word for the Danish language.
